Keeping Your YouTube Downloader Updated

If you are not on a rolling release distro and are having a hard time keeping yt-dlp or other youtube-dl fork up to date then I have the solution.

For this guide I'm using 

yt-dlp
. First check your path with 
echo $PATH
. Most distros will have 
~/.local/bin
at or near the top; above 
/usr/bin
or whatever other path that 
yt-dlp
was installed in. If you don't have 
~/.local/bin
then make it. Then go to the yt-dlp releases and the the executable for your OS. Put that executable in your 
~/.local/bin
directory.

Now when mpv looks for the yt-dlp executable it will check each directory in your path starting with your 

~/.local/bin
directory with the new yt-dlp executable. Problem solved.

If your executable gets out of date just grab the new one and put it in 

~/.local/bin
.
