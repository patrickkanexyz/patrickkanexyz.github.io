On The Flipper Zero

I've had a flipper for some time now. When I first got it I played around with it, cloned some gate fobs, messed with turning peoples' TV's on and off, and that was about it.

Then I started looking into what other apps I could run on the flipper. Browsing the 'app store' it looked like mostly games and largely useless utilities.

This initial impression of the flipper left me disapointed by the flipper, writing it off as a nerd toy for Redditors. But, then I found out about UART and that has caused me to take another look at the flip. The rest of this page will detail the useful and interesting things I find to do with my flipper.
