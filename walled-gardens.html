Walled Gardens

Walled gardens are bad. That is all.

Walled gardens require almost by definition a kind of gatekeeping. They also have a leveling effect of the population; you can only have a predetermined set of ideas and techneques. This is very boring. Finally, once you are in them you cannot easily move out of them. Very annoying.

Walled gardens have some use, as far as communities, but should be avoided in regards to technology.
